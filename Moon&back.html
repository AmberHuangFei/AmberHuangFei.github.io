    • Long-distance relationship can be hard

    Long-distance relationship can be a hassle,especially during a global pendemic. Me and my team identified solutions to bring long-distance lovers closer.My major responsibilies during this collaboration are focused on formulating evidence-based design solution.

    Through close examination and testing we discovered the simple recipes that would spice up long distance relationship. Moon&Back is an application that can help bring joy and intimacy to couples and reduce the anxiety brought by distance.

    What is the problem

    Survey

    In a short time spam we try to understand the context of the problem we're facing. We design a survey and sent to people who had experience with long-distance relationship to get closer to the pain points. Luckily we received 23 response in the end of the survey here’s some of our major discovery:

    Lack of intimacy

    Inabilities to meetup

    Miss communication

    Key insights
  • Long distance relationship lack intimacy and closeness, that lead to more one side needing more control and feeling of security.
  • Long distance couples don't need more communication they need effective commmunication.
  • Existing solution forentertainment can be improved and catering to long distance needs.
  • Long-distance relationship require more affection and personalized way to feel affectionate and motivation.
    • User Needs
  • Provide long distance lover privacy and their personal space to define their own intimacy.
  • Motivate long-distance lover to connect and resolve their miscommunication.
  • Design more fun and interactive game or activities for long-distance lover to bring more excitement and freshness.
  • Personalized service or features need to be include for lovers to express their love to express their affections.

    • Personas & User Journey

    We build two sets of persona based on our insight to capture the major user needs, and making sure we all stay on the same page when we ideating for the final solution.

    Our Solution

    A swiss army knife for long distance relationships to chat, archive your memories, play games and Level up your love in from of a mobile app

    Space for Lover

    We designed function that will help improve communication between loved one. Seriously what can be more important then avoiding miscommunication?

    "Love you to The Moon & Back"

    Chat with your Long Distance Loved one in a meaningful way all in one place

    “There will always be sunshine & Rainbow in our chatrooms”

    Share your current mood status to let your partner know you're too exhausted to talk or feeling flirty. Horrible sweet talker? We got ideas for you :)

    Fun & Making memories

    Moon&Back provide lover with activity to hang out together. We understand the pain not to see your loved one but why not have a virtual date?

    “VideoCall &Have fun”

    Video call can be more than small talks! Play a quick game with your loved ones.

    "Schedulean Online Date"

    Don't have any online date ideas? We got you covered with a list of activities!

    "Level Up Your Love"

    How in love are you this week? We provide suggestions, rewards for leveling up & stats. Plus, take care of your virtual puppy together

This comment had help me in my future design process and makeing sure I avoid functionailty that are not bring postive effect to my users directly.
