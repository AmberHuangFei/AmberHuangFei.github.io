    Project background

    Plip is a project made for Adobe creative Jam competition 2021. We created this concept project to help creative writers and digital artists to find inspiration,  organize their thoughts, and get first-hand information on events that might interest them.

    Challenge

    One-stop community platform that encourages users to discover new interests. We first narrow down the problem by coming up with an HMW statement.

    HMW create a single-stop platform for creative writers to share, inspire, find accurate resources & support as a community.

    Research

    Design

    Analyze user needs, expectations, and frustrations.

    Interview

    Define details on pain points, understand their work flows.

    Analyze

    Understanding what works and what doesn’t on the existing solutions.

    The research process for this project had two major parts:

    Primary research: Reach out to creative writers online (exp. Reddit creative writer channel.) By connecting with them, we can understand the real problem they are struggling with during their writing process.

    Secondary Research: Our secondary research include competitor analysis and method to break information bubbles. By engaging these research methods, we gained a basic idea of the structure of our platform.

    Insight

    Based on our research result we had come up with these insights.

    Insight#1

    Getting inspired and organize new ideas are challenging for creative writers.

    Insight#2

    Daydreaming is an essential tool for creative users to come up with inspiration.

    Insight#3

    Text and messaging is the favoured way to share insights between writers community.

    Insight#4

    Writers are exclusive during writing, but feedback and responses are helpful.

    Insight#5

    Image and music are vital media to stimulate creative thinking for writers.

    Insight#6

    Writers look for the community to support each other and gain first-hand insights.
